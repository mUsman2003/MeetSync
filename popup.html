ℹ️ How to capture everything

MeetSync relies on Google Meet's interface. To capture messages and captions, you must enable them in the Meet window.

1
Open the Chat Panel
Click the 💬 Chat icon in the bottom right toolbar. MeetSync can only record messages while this panel is open.
2
Turn on Captions
Click the 🔠 CC icon in the bottom toolbar. Live transcription will appear in the Transcription tab.
3
Background Recording
The extension will continue to record chat and captions even if you switch tabs, as long as Meet remains open.
