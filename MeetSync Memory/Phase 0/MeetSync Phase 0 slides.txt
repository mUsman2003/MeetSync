MeetSync
The Problem: Critical verbal decisions and tasks are
frequently lost or misunderstood.
Context: Occurs during live, remote, or informal
discussions where tasks are assigned "on the fly".
Friction: Passive participation goes unnoticed due to a
lack of real-time engagement feedback.

Who Has This Problem?
Primary Users:
Project Leads
Managers tracking commitments.
Secondary Stakeholders:
Individual affected by ownership and rework.

Requirement:

Moving beyond generic "users" to identify those feeling
direct pain.

Current Behavior & Workarounds
01 Existing Tools: Incomplete manual note-taking or tedious
re-watching of long recordings
02 Manual Processes: Relying on "trust" and informal
memory of verbal agreements.
03 The Failure: Current workarounds are time-consuming,
prone to human error, and lack engagement tracking.

Why it matters and Product Intuition
The Cost: Missed deadlines, high stress, and
uncompensated scope expansion.

High-Level Intuition: Real-time engagement checkpoints
using ASR, NLP, and LLM intent extraction.
